Why Vaccinate?

Vaccines are crucial for protecting your pet from highly contagious and potentially fatal diseases. They help build immunity, ensuring your furry friend stays healthy and active for years to come. Following a schedule keeps their protection strong.

Dog Care

Grooming Schedule

Recommended routine to keep your friend happy and healthy.

Brushing

Every 2 to 3 days

Helps remove loose fur, dirt, and prevents matting. Great bonding time!

Bathing

Every 4 to 6 weeks

Use dog-specific shampoo. Avoid over-bathing to protect their natural skin oils.

Nail Trimming

Once a month

If you hear clicking on the floor, it's time for a trim. Be careful not to cut the quick.

Teeth Cleaning

Daily or every other day

Crucial for long-term health. Use enzymatic toothpaste designed for dogs.
