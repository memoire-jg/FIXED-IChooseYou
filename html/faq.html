Frequently Asked Questions

Find gentle guidance and answers to help you care for your new companion.

Most adult cats do well with two measured meals a day. Kittens usually need smaller meals more often.

Start with one quiet room, let your cat explore slowly, and keep food, water, litter, and hiding spots easy to reach.

Scratching is natural. Offer sturdy scratchers near favorite furniture and reward your cat when they use them.
